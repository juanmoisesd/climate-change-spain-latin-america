This dataset provides a comprehensive collection of climate and environmental indicators for Spain and Latin America covering the period from 2000 to 2024. It includes temperature anomalies, CO2 concentration levels from the Izana Observatory, greenhouse gas emissions, sea level rise data, and specific impacts such as Andean glacier retreat and Amazon deforestation. The data is intended for researchers, policy makers, and educators interested in the regional impacts of climate change in the Ibero-American context.
