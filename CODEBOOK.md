# Codebook

This document describes the variables and methodology used in this dataset.

## Indicators

### CO2 Emissions
Emissions of carbon dioxide in tonnes per capita.
- **Source:** Global Carbon Budget / National reports.
- **Unit:** Metric tonnes.

### Temperature Anomaly
Annual mean temperature anomaly relative to the 1961-1990 baseline.
- **Source:** AEMET (Spain), Copernicus/National Met Services (Latin America).
- **Unit:** Degrees Celsius (°C).

### Glacier Retreat
Percentage of glacier area loss compared to 1986 baseline.
- **Source:** SENAMHI (Bolivia/Peru).
- **Unit:** Percentage (%).
