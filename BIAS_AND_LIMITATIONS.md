# Bias and Limitations

- **Geographic Bias:** The dataset focuses on Spain and specific Latin American countries. Data for small islands or central American nations may be less comprehensive.
- **Temporal Limitations:** Detailed monthly data is only available for specific indicators (e.g., Izaña CO2), while others are annual.
- **Reporting Variations:** Different countries may have slightly different methodologies for measuring GHG emissions or glacier retreat.
