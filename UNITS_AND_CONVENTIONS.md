# Units and Conventions

- **Temperature:** All temperature anomalies are in degrees Celsius (°C) relative to the 1961-1990 reference period unless otherwise specified.
- **CO2 Concentrations:** Parts per million (ppm).
- **CO2 Emissions:** Metric tonnes per capita.
- **Glacier Area:** Percentage loss (%) since 1986.
- **Date Format:** YYYY or YYYY-MM-DD.
- **Geographic Scope:** Spain and Spanish-speaking Latin American countries + Brazil.
