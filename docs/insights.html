Actionable Insights

How is the Ocean Heat Content (OHC) evolving?

Ocean heat content has shown a consistent upward trend. Oceans absorb over 90% of the excess heat trapped in the atmosphere, making this a critical indicator of long-term global warming.

Is the rate of renewable energy adoption accelerating?

While absolute values vary by region, the growth rate of renewable energy capacity has increased significantly since 2015, driven by falling costs in solar and wind technologies.

What is the relationship between GDP climate risk and actual losses?

Economic exposure to climate risk (GDP) is translating into higher insurance losses. The data suggests a correlation between extreme weather frequency and economic impact.
